Detection fixture: localStorage signals

This page sets localStorage keys that match known service signatures (ajs_user_id, ajs_anonymous_id, _hjSessionId) so the scanner can detect and classify them. No real third-party scripts or network calls are made.

  • ajs_user_id — Segment (analytics)
  • ajs_anonymous_id — Segment (analytics)
  • _hjSessionId — Hotjar (analytics)
